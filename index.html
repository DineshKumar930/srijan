Our Mission

At सृजन Foundation, we believe that every child in India deserves access to quality education and coaching, regardless of their economic background. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap by providing free & subsidized coaching to underprivileged students and also a holistic development of the student to compete in their generation.

We focus on holistic development that goes beyond academics, nurturing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and confidence in our students. Our team of dedicated educators and volunteers work tirelessly to create an environment where students can thrive and realize their full potential.

Since our inception in 2021, we have impacted over 1,000 students across 3 districts in UP India.

Your Thoughts on Indian Education

We believe Indian education needs to evolve beyond rote learning and focus more on critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. The current system creates unnecessary stress while often failing to prepare students for real-world challenges.

At सृजन Foundation, we're working to change this by:

  • Incorporating project-based learning in our curriculum
  • Teaching financial literacy and life skills
  • Focusing on mental health and stress management
  • Using technology to make learning more interactive

What changes would you like to see in Indian education? Share your thoughts with us!
