Sarah Johnson

Founder & CEO

Sarah founded our organization in 2010 after witnessing firsthand the educational disparities in underprivileged communities. With a Master's in Education Policy and 15 years of nonprofit experience, she leads our strategic vision and ensures we stay true to our mission of free education for all.

Under her leadership, we've expanded to serve over 10,000 students annually across 5 regions. Sarah believes education is the most powerful tool for social change.

David Chen

Education Director

David brings 12 years of classroom experience and curriculum development expertise to our team. He designs our learning programs to be accessible, engaging, and effective for students from diverse backgrounds.

With a PhD in Educational Psychology, David ensures our teaching methods are evidence-based and tailored to each student's needs. He also leads our teacher training programs.

Priya builds relationships with communities to identify students who would benefit most from our programs. Fluent in four languages, she helps bridge cultural and linguistic barriers to education access.
